> I would add one more dimension to your framework: **temporal isolation**. Tests that depend on timing — async operations, rate limits, or scheduled tasks — introduce another coupling layer that often goes unnoticed until production.
>
> In CI/CD pipelines, I have seen test suites pass at 2 AM but fail at 9 AM due to background job schedules. This is order independence in the temporal dimension.
